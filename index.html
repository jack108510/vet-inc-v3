Inflation Catch-Up

Service codes that haven't been repriced in years are below market value. The AI scans every code, identifies which ones have fallen behind, and brings them up to where they should be.

99 Cent Pricing

Round prices to .99 or .95. The most basic retail technique — and most vet clinics haven't done it. Across thousands of transactions, one cent per service adds up to real money without a single client noticing.

Margin Growth

The AI doesn't raise everything at once. It selects the optimal time and amount for each individual service code based on utilization, seasonality, and client behavior. One code goes up 3% on a Tuesday, another waits until next quarter. The system monitors each change independently and self-corrects.
