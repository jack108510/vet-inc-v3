How It Works
Campaigns That Find Money You're Leaving on the Table
Vet INC runs continuous campaigns on your data. Each targets a specific type of revenue loss and shows exactly what to fix, what to charge, and how much you'll recover.
Campaign 1
Inflation Adjuster
Compounds CPI from each item's last price change. Shows which services have fallen behind inflation and what they should cost now — item by item, dollar by dollar.
Real date tracking per item
Annual uplift calculated per SKU
Campaign 2
.99 Pricing Recovery
Rounds every non-.99 price up. Uses actual transaction volume to show exactly how much revenue was left behind — and what you'd gain going forward.
Based on real transaction history
Zero impact on patient perception
Campaign 3
Inventory Micro Margin
When a supplier raises costs and you update the selling price, the system adds an extra 5% margin bump. Tracks units sold and shows what that earns annually.
Auto-detects supplier cost changes
Margin stacked on top of pass-through
Revenue Growth
Exam Drop Initiative
Lower exam fees to attract new patients, then recover the gap by bumping the top pairing items that always follow. The system calculates the exact trade-off so you never lose money — you gain volume.
The Drop
Lower Exam Prices
Reduce exam fees to remove the barrier for new pet owners. The system shows which exams to drop and by how much, based on your local market and volume data.
The Bump
Pairing Price Recovery
Every exam pairs with 2–3 services (vaccines, bloodwork, etc.). Small bumps on these high-attachment items recover the exam drop and then some — net positive from day one.
