99 Cent Pricing

Round prices to .99 or .95. The most basic retail technique — and most vet clinics haven't done it. Across thousands of transactions, one cent per service adds up to real money without a single client noticing.

Margin Growth

The AI doesn't raise everything at once. It selects the optimal time and amount for each individual service code based on utilization, seasonality, and client behavior. One code goes up 3% on a Tuesday, another waits until next quarter. Each change is monitored independently and self-corrects.

Loss Leader Optimization

Drop prices on high-visibility shoppable services — exams, neuters, vaccines — to drive more clients through the door. Capture margin on the procedures and follow-up services that follow.
