How it works

Automated campaigns that find money.

Each campaign targets a specific type of revenue loss. Zero manual work.

Campaign 1

Inflation Adjuster

Tracks CPI from each item's last price change. Shows exactly which services have fallen behind inflation and what they should cost now.

Campaign 2

.99 Pricing Recovery

Flags every non-.99 price, calculates the gap based on actual transaction volume, and shows annual recovery potential.

Campaign 3

Inventory Micro Margin

When supplier costs rise and you update pricing, the system stacks an extra 5% margin on top automatically.

Revenue Growth

Exam Drop Initiative

Lower exam fees to attract new patients, then recover the gap by adjusting the high-attachment services that always follow. Net positive from day one.

The Drop

Lower Exams

System identifies which exams to drop and by how much, based on local market data.

The Bump

Pairing Recovery

Every exam pairs with 2-3 services. Small bumps on these high-attachment items recover the drop and then some.

Always On

The Vet INC Brain

Monitors your prices, expenses, and transactions 24/7. Surfaces recommendations before you know there's a problem.

Continuous Scanning

Every charge code, cost change, and fee schedule tracked against benchmarks and your own history.

Expense Alerts

Detects abnormal spending, over-staffing, supply cost creep, and policy drift automatically.
